Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: December 1, 2025

In accordance with Facebook Platform rules, we provide a way for users to request that their data be deleted.

Data Storage Policy

Chat Bot does not store any personal data on its servers. All messages are processed in real-time and are not persisted in any database.

How to Request Confirmation

If you wish to confirm that we do not hold your data, or if you have any concerns, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to barangantrip@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request.

We will respond to your request within 30 days to confirm that no data is held.
